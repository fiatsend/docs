## ugxFIAT - A Ugandan Shilling-Backed Token

**ugxFIAT** represents the total amount of Ugandan Shillings in Fiatsend's reserves and is burnt upon settlement completion.

---

### How does ugxFIAT work?

#### **Creation of ugxFIAT**

To mint ugxFIAT:

- Users supply equivalent UGX into reserves as liquidity.
- Contributors earn attractive APYs and **FSEND** token rewards.
- Deposited UGX is converted into ugxFIAT tokens at a 1:1 ratio, ensuring full collateral backing and stability.

#### **Collateral Backing**

- **Transparency**: ugxFIAT is fully backed by fiat reserves held in secure accounts.
- **Verification**: zk-proofs and audits verify the collateral and maintain user trust.

---

#### **Using ugxFIAT for Payments and Offramping**

1. **Fiat Offramping**  
   ugxFIAT enables quick settlement into local accounts while holding minimal on-chain value.

2. **Tokenized Payments**  
   Utilize ugxFIAT for efficient and borderless transactions on supported platforms.

---

### Redemption of ugxFIAT

- ugxFIAT can be redeemed at any time for an equivalent amount of UGX.
- The redemption process ensures liquidity and easy access to fiat currency.
