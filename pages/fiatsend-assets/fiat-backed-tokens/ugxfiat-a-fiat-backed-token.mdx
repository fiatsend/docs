# Fiatsend Assets

## ugxFIAT - A Ugandan Shilling-Backed Token

Your Gateway to Stable, Tokenized Payments and Seamless Fiat Offramping

---

### What is ugxFIAT?

ugxFIAT is a stable token backed by the Ugandan Shilling (UGX), designed to simplify fiat-to-crypto and crypto-to-fiat transactions. As a ugxFIAT holder, you can access fast, secure, and cost-efficient tokenized payments, enabling seamless fiat offramping and real-world integration.

---

### How does ugxFIAT work?

#### **Creation of ugxFIAT**

Users can mint ugxFIAT by depositing an equivalent amount of UGX through Fiatsend's integration. Each token is minted on a 1:1 basis, ensuring every ugxFIAT token is fully backed by fiat reserves.

#### **Collateral Backing**

Every ugxFIAT token is backed by reserves held in trusted financial institutions, ensuring security and stability for users.

---

#### **Using ugxFIAT for Payments and Offramping**

1. **Fiat Offramping**  
   Convert ugxFIAT into UGX directly through Fiatsend's services. Receive the equivalent amount in your mobile wallet or bank account.

2. **Tokenized Payments**  
   Use ugxFIAT for fast and borderless transactions across decentralized platforms and payment networks.

---

### Redemption of ugxFIAT

ugxFIAT tokens can be redeemed at any time for the equivalent amount of UGX. This ensures liquidity and instant access to fiat when needed.
