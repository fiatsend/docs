# Fiatsend Assets

## ghsFIAT - A Ghanaian Cedi-Backed Token

**ghsFIAT** represents the total amount of Ghanaian Cedis (GHS) in Fiatsend's reserves and is burnt upon settlement completion.

---

### How does ghsFIAT work?

#### **Creation of ghsFIAT**

To mint ghsFIAT:

- Users supply equivalent GHS into reserves in the form of liquidity.
- Contributors earn competitive APYs and incentives in the form of **FSEND** tokens.
- Deposited GHS is converted into ghsFIAT tokens on a 1:1 ratio, ensuring stability and full collateral backing.

#### **Collateral Backing**

- **Transparency**: ghsFIAT is fully backed by fiat reserves held in trusted accounts.
- **Verification**: zk-proofs and regular audits verify the 1:1 backing of every ghsFIAT token.

---

#### **Using ghsFIAT for Payments and Offramping**

1. **Fiat Offramping**  
   ghsFIAT facilitates offramping into settlement accounts and does not hold intrinsic on-chain value.

2. **Tokenized Payments**  
   Use ghsFIAT for fast, borderless transactions across supported platforms.

---

### Redemption of ghsFIAT

- ghsFIAT can be redeemed at any time for an equivalent amount of GHS.
- The redemption process ensures liquidity and easy access to fiat.

---

- **Fiat Onramping and Offramping**: Seamlessly transition between fiat and crypto.

---

### Getting Started with ghsFIAT

1. **Mint ghsFIAT**: Deposit GHS through Fiatsend to instantly create ghsFIAT tokens.
2. **Use ghsFIAT**: Utilize it for tokenized payments, decentralized finance (DeFi) opportunities, or fiat conversions.
3. **Redeem ghsFIAT**: Convert your ghsFIAT tokens back to fiat through Fiatsend's trusted offramping services.
