## **Merchants (Businesses & Payment Processors)**

Merchants integrate Fiatsend as a **payment gateway** to accept stablecoin payments and receive fiat instantly. This allows businesses to accept crypto payments without worrying about volatility or complicated blockchain integrations.

### **Key Features for Merchants:**

- ✅ **Instant Crypto-to-Fiat Settlements**: Receive payments in fiat automatically.
- ✅ **Low Transaction Fees**: Cost-effective payment processing compared to traditional providers.
- ✅ **Multi-Chain Payment Support**: Accept stablecoins from different blockchains.
- ✅ **Seamless API Integration**: Easy integration into existing e-commerce platforms.
- ✅ **KYC & Compliance Solutions**: Ensures regulatory adherence for businesses.

### **Example Use Cases:**

- 🛒 Online stores accepting **USDC, USDT, or cUSD** while receiving **local fiat** directly.
- 👩‍💻 Gig workers and freelancers using Fiatsend for **global payments** without middlemen.
- 🏪 Physical merchants using **QR codes** for seamless in-person payments.
