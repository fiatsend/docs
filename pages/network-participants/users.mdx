## **Users (End Consumers)**

Users are individuals who leverage Fiatsend for **fast, secure, and cost-effective** fiat-crypto transactions. Whether they need to **cash out digital assets**, **send remittances**, or **pay for goods and services**, Fiatsend provides a seamless experience with minimal friction.

### **Key Features for Users:**

- ✅ **Instant Fiat & Crypto Transactions**: Convert and transfer funds in real time.
- ✅ **Multiple Onboarding Options**: Sign up with a **wallet, email (magic link), or social authentication**.
- ✅ **Multi-Currency Support**: Transact in stablecoins and fiat currencies.
- ✅ **On-Chain Identity & Account Abstraction**: Simplifies Web3 interactions while ensuring security.
- ✅ **Cross-Border Payments**: Enable global transactions with low fees.
- ✅ **Rewards & Incentives**: Earn points for engaging with the network, completing quests, and participating in governance.
