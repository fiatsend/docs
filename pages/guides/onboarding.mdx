# How to Use Fiatsend

## For New Users

1. **Sign Up**: Use your wallet or email to register.
2. **Complete KYC**: Verify your identity for higher transaction limits.

## For Existing Users

- Deposit stablecoins to **fiatsend.eth**.
- Use GHSFIAT tokens to withdraw fiat.

## Setting Spending Limits

Increase limits by completing higher levels of KYC.

## Address Personalization

Claim a unique address (`<username>.fiatsend.eth`) for transactions.
