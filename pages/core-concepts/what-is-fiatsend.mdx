# What is Fiatsend?

FiatSend is designed to simplify cross-border money transfers, ensuring a seamless experience for our users. Whether you're sending money for business or personal reasons, FiatSend has you covered.

The thought process is as follows:
- Sending money accross borders is expensive.
- We believe sending money should be simple and fast
- Users must readily has access and full control to their funds all the time regardless of whether the platform is up or down

## Our Approach:

- Effortless Transactions: Experience a straightforward process from start to finish.
- Reliable and Secure: Trust in a secure platform that prioritizes the safety of your transactions.
- Global Accessibility: Reach recipients worldwide with our extensive network.
