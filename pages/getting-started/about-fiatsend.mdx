# Getting Started

## What is Fiatsend?

Fiatsend is a platform that enables instant conversion of stablecoins into local fiat currencies, starting with Ghanaian Cedis (GHS). It bridges traditional and crypto finance by simplifying transactions for users of all levels.

## Who is Fiatsend For?

- **Crypto-savvy users**: Those familiar with wallet connections and blockchain.
- **Traditional users**: Individuals new to crypto who prefer email-based sign-ups and simpler onboarding.

## How it Works

1. **Sign Up**: Create an account using your wallet or email.
2. **Complete KYC**: Verify your identity to unlock higher transaction limits.
3. **Deposit Stablecoins**: Send USDC or USDT to Fiatsend.
4. **Receive GHS**: Funds are instantly processed to your mobile money wallet.
