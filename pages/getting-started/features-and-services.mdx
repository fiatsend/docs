# Features and Services

## User Onboarding

- **Wallet or Email Sign-Up**: Choose between connecting your wallet or creating an email-based account.
- **Embedded Wallets**: Email sign-ups automatically create wallets for new users.

## KYC Process

- **Level 1**: Limit of GHS 100,000 per month.
- **Level 2**: Limit of GHS 500,000 per month.

## Stablecoin Transactions

- Deposit USDC or USDT to the Fiatsend platform via **fiatsend.eth**.

## Mobile Money Integration

Link your mobile wallet to withdraw fiat directly.

## Tokenized Transactions

GHSFIAT tokens represent fiat securely for in-app transactions.

## Rewards and Engagement

- Burn GHSFIAT tokens to redeem NFTs.
- Earn rewards based on transaction activity.
