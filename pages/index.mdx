# Fiatsend Network

## Introduction

Fiatsend is a decentralized financial network that bridges traditional finance with Web3, enabling seamless, real-time fiat-to-stablecoin and stablecoin-to-fiat transactions. Our mission is to make digital payments faster, more secure, and globally accessible by integrating blockchain technology with financial services.
