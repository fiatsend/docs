# FAQ

## General

**Q: What currencies are supported?**  
A: Currently, we support USDC and USDT.

**Q: What is the transaction fee?**  
A: Minimal fees are charged per transaction, and refunds protect against rate fluctuations.

## KYC

**Q: What documents are required?**  
A: A government-issued ID and a selfie for verification.

**Q: Can I use Fiatsend without KYC?**  
A: Yes, but transaction limits are capped at GHS 1,000 (one-time).

## Rewards

**Q: How do airdrops work?**  
A: Rewards are based on metrics like transaction volume and frequency.
