# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## What happens if I lose my DID?

If you lose access to your **Decentralized Identifier (DID)**, you can recover it using your **backup passphrase**. It's essential to keep your backup passphrase secure and accessible.

## What are the transaction fees for cross-border payments?

Fiatsend minimizes transaction fees by leveraging **decentralized web nodes** and **Web5 technology**. The exact fees depend on the network used, but we strive to keep them as low as possible for our users.

## Can I connect Fiatsend to other Web5 applications?

Absolutely! Fiatsend allows users to connect with other **Web5-enabled applications** using their **decentralized credentials**. This interoperability enhances your experience and expands your options within the Web5 ecosystem.
